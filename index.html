Hello! My name is Shweta Jain. I am a Computing Innovation Fellow at the University of Utah, working with Prof. Blair Sullivan. Prior to this, I was a postdoc at the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign working with Prof. Hanghang Tong. I obtained a PhD in Computer Science from the University of California, Santa Cruz, where I was advised by Prof. Seshadhri Comandur. My research interests are in randomized and approximation algorithms, combinatorial optimization, graph mining, and algorithms for massive data. I am grateful to have received the SIGKDD Best Dissertation Runner-Up Award for 2021!
